Hey, I'm Isaac Benaka

The ocean environment has fascinated me since my childhood and that early curiosity led me to pursue a career in coastal and ocean science, where I now work as a researcher and drone pilot at NOAA’s Northeast Fisheries Science Center. I’m fascinated by the new perspectives drones offer and love using them to advance research and conservation. A priority of mine is striving to streamline research processes to make science more accessible and efficient.
